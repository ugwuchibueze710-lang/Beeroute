# 🐝 BeeRoute

## Your AI Driving Companion

BeeRoute is a next-generation navigation system that combines **offline-first mapping**, **real-time intelligent routing**, and an **always-available AI driving companion**.

Instead of just showing directions, BeeRoute understands your journey, talks with you naturally, and adapts your route in real time based on your needs.

---

## 🚗 Vision

Traditional navigation apps show you where to go.

BeeRoute understands *how you want to travel*.

It is designed to feel like an intelligent co-pilot that:

- Guides you through every turn
- Answers questions while driving
- Adjusts routes through natural conversation
- Suggests stops like gas, food, or rest
- Learns your preferences over time

---

## 🧠 Core Concept

BeeRoute combines:

- 🗺️ Offline-first navigation system (OpenStreetMap-based)
- 🤖 Local AI assistant (Ollama-compatible or on-device models)
- 🎙️ Voice-driven interaction (hands-free driving)
- 🧭 Smart multi-stop routing
- 🚦 Context-aware travel assistance

---

## ✨ Key Features (Planned)

### Navigation
- Turn-by-turn directions
- Multi-stop trip planning
- Highway / backroad / scenic route modes
- Offline map support
- Dynamic rerouting

### AI Driving Companion
- Natural conversation while driving
- Route changes via voice
- Travel explanations (geography, landmarks, etc.)
- Personality modes (calm, funny, professional, etc.)

### Voice System
- Wake word activation (optional)
- Speech-to-text
- Text-to-speech responses
- Fully hands-free control

### Smart Travel Assistance
- Gas station recommendations
- Food and coffee stops
- Rest area suggestions
- Traffic and hazard awareness (when available)

---

## 🏗️ Architecture (Planned)

BeeRoute is built as a modular system:

- `navigation/` → Routing engine
- `ai/` → Local AI brain
- `voice/` → Speech system
- `maps/` → Offline map data & rendering
- `ui/` → Futuristic driving interface
- `core/` → App logic & coordination

Each module is independent and replaceable.

---

## ⚙️ Technology Goals
